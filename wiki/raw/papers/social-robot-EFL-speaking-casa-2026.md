# Social Robot for EFL Speaking Practice — CASA Paradigm

**arXiv:** 2604.12789v1 | **Published:** 2026-04-14 | **Cats:** cs.HC
**Authors:** Yiran Du, Jinlong Li, Huimin He, Chenghao Wang, Bin Zou
**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.12789v1

## Abstract

This study investigates Chinese primary school students' acceptance of a social robot for English-as-a-foreign-language (EFL) speaking practice through a sequential explanatory mixed-methods design. Integrating the Technology Acceptance Model (TAM) and the Computers Are Social Actors (CASA) paradigm, the research explores both functional and social factors influencing learners' behavioural intention to use the robot. Quantitative data from 436 students were analysed using structural equation modelling, followed by qualitative interviews with twelve students to interpret the findings.

## Key Findings

- **最强预测因子:** perceived enjoyment（感知愉悦性）和 ease of use（易用性）
- **社会属性显著增强愉悦感:** warmth（温暖感）、anthropomorphism（拟人化）、social presence（社会存在感）
- **感知智能影响有用性，但不直接影响易用性**
- 情感和社会参与是年轻学习者接受教育机器人的核心

## 设计启示

为EFL口语练习设计教育机器人时，**情感和社会参与**比纯功能性更重要：
- 机器人应具备温暖感和拟人化特征
- 社会存在感设计可提升学习动机和口语自信

## 理论框架

TAM + CASA（计算机是社会行动者）= 功能性因素 + 社会性因素共同决定接受度
